What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on the user's device when visiting a website. These files contain information that is used to enhance the browsing experience, analyze site usage, and personalize content.

Types of Cookies Used

  1. Technical Cookies: These cookies are essential to enable the Site to function properly and provide services requested by the user. They do not require user consent.
  2. Analytical Cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Site by collecting anonymous information about visited pages, time spent on the Site, and other statistical data. We use this information to improve the quality and usability of our Site.
  3. Third-Party Cookies: The Site may include content or services provided by third parties, which may use their own cookies. These cookies are subject to the respective third party's privacy policies.

Managing Cookies
